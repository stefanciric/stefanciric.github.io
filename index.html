Basic Features

Subcontrol will help you to track your subscriptions efficiently. You can set reminders and get notified before the cancellation deadline. Cancel your subscription after being notified with one click and don't waste time waiting in the queue at the post office to send the cancellation letter for a small fee.

Premium Features

Subcontrol takes care of everything for you. Besides tracking your subscriptions and reminding you when to cancel, you won't have any fees for postal cancellation letters. As a premium user you will get personalized offers to save costs for the same conditions as in your current subscription. In the near future you will have the ability to pay your subscriptions directly in the Subcontrol applications and a personal budget plan. The budget plan will be created with Artificial Intelligence.
